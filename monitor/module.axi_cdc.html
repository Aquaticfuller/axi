Module axi_cdc

A clock domain crossing on an AXI interface.

For each of the five AXI channels, this module instantiates a CDC FIFO, whose push and pop ports are in separate clock domains. IMPORTANT: For each AXI channel, you MUST properly constrain three paths through the FIFO; see the header of cdc_fifo_gray for instructions.
